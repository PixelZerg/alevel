---
title: 6.5 - Boolean Algebra
---

- like Maths Algebra - follow certain rules

- Boolean operators

  - $$+$$ addition
  - $$\cdot$$ multiplaction
  - $$\overline{}$$ not (inversion)

- NB: AND has greater precedence than OR

- Boolean Identities:

  - $$A+0=A$$
  - $$A+A=A$$
  - $$0\cdot A=0$$
  - $$A\cdot A=A$$
  - $$\overline{\overline{A}}=A$$
  - $$A+1=1$$
  - $$A+\overline{A}=1$$
  - $$1\cdot A=A$$
  - $$A\cdot \overline{A}=0$$

- Boolean laws:

  - Commutative
    - $$AB$$ = $$BA$$
    - $$A+B=B+A$$
  - associative
    - $$A(BC)=(AB)C$$
    - $$A+(B+C)=(A+B)+C$$
  - distributive
    - $$A(B+C)=AB+AC$$
    - $$A+(BC)=(A+B)(A+C)$$
